4. Ask Questions That Reveal Compatibility

A perfect partner for you should share compatible views on a few key topics:

  • How they handle conflict and communication.
  • How they balance work, family, and free time.
  • What they think about commitment, loyalty, and money.

You can learn a lot by asking simple questions like "What does a good weekend look like for you?" or "What are you working on improving about yourself this year?"

5. Test Consistency, Not Perfection

The right partner will not be perfect, but they will be consistent:

  • They follow through on plans or communicate when something changes.
  • They respect your boundaries and time.
